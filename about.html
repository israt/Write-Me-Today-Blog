About Heidi

In 2015 I relocated from my Walbundrie farm of 15 years to the surrounds of Albury NSW.

My background is farming, business and taxation law. I had a career in the Tax Office for many years. Now I'm a volunteer at a Cancer Council information centre; a Lifeline phone counsellor & I also have a business that runs suicide prevention workshops.

I have changed and grown through multiple journeys of family with cancer plus sudden death.

I am a member of the Australian Counselling Association.

Qualifications

  • Diploma of Counselling
  • Certificate IV Telephone Counselling
  • Mental Health First Aid (MHFA)
  • Applied Suicide Intervention Skills Training (ASIST)
  • Bachelor of Business (Accountancy)
  • Master of Taxation

More

I am passionate about suicide prevention and providing service to those who can’t get it face to face. I believe everyone has a story and deserves to be heard. I have a blue heeler, a couple of horses and lots of birds. You would not believe what music I listen to.
